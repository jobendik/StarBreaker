# STARBREAKER — Game Description

## Tagline
Survive the swarm. Evolve your arsenal. Break the Void Titan.

## Short description (≈ 1 line)
A fast-paced arcade survival shooter: dodge endless swarms, level up and evolve
your weapons, and destroy the Void Titan.

## Medium description (store blurb)
STARBREAKER is a fast-paced, single-player arcade survival shooter played on a
single screen. Pilot your starfighter through relentless waves of enemies, vacuum
up cores to level up, and choose powerful upgrades each level. Chain your weapons
into evolved super-weapons, dash through danger, and hold out long enough to face
— and break — the Void Titan. Earn cores between runs to unlock new ships, skins,
and permanent upgrades.

## Long description
Drop straight into the action: every run is a frantic test of survival against an
ever-growing swarm. As you destroy enemies they drop cores; collect them to level
up and pick from a hand of randomized upgrade cards — more damage, faster fire,
extra projectiles, crit, dash charges, and more. Stack the right combination and
your weapons **evolve** into screen-clearing super-weapons.

Push deeper through escalating sectors until a **Void Titan** boss appears. Beat it
for a big bounty and the choice to **finish the run** banked, or enter **Overdrive**
for a higher-stakes, higher-reward continuation.

When you fall, you can **revive** — either with a free Backup Reactor charge or by
watching a rewarded ad — and jump straight back into the fight. Cores you bank
carry over between runs to unlock additional ships, cosmetic skins, weapons, and a
tree of permanent upgrades. A rotating set of **daily missions** and **daily cargo**
keeps giving you reasons to come back.

### Key features
- ⚡ Instant-action, single-screen survival shooter — no downtime
- 🔫 Level-up draft: randomized upgrade cards every level
- 🧬 Weapon **evolutions** into powerful super-weapons
- 🛸 Multiple unlockable **ships** and cosmetic **skins**
- 🌳 Persistent meta-progression: cores, permanent upgrades, records
- 👹 **Void Titan** boss fights and an Overdrive endless mode
- 📆 Daily missions and daily cargo rewards
- 📱 Plays on desktop and mobile (keyboard or touch)
